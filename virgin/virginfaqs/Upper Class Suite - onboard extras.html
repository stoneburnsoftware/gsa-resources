Upper Class Suite - onboard extras Published 10/06/2008 05.56 PM | Updated 22/10/2014 05.11 PM What other extras can I enjoy on board? Whilst relaxing in the longest fully flat bed in business, the Upper Class suite comes with lots of extras. Reclining leather seat. An onboard bar. Your personal 10.4" wide screen TV. In-seat power An ottoman for storage space. (This can also be used as a seat for your travel partner or guest). A large manoeuverable table.
